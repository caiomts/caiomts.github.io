How to maintain a reliable supply of bikes regardless of changes in demand?

In this project I build a highly interpretable model to predict the demand for bikes, as well as understand the main attributes responsible for its fluctuation.

Keywords: Python, Machine Learning, Supervised Learning, Regression, Pandas, Numpy, Scikit-Learn, matplotlib, seaborn, deployed with github-pages.
