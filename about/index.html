About Me

November 1, 2021   

Hi There!

I’m Caio and I’m passionate about open source projects, programming, data science and machine learning. But I also love camping, traveling, running, cycling, reading and hanging out with friends and learning about other cultures.

About three year ago I decided to change my career. I got a sabbatical year, moved abroad and started to learn another language. A bit after these changes I finally dive into data science and programming - I’d had contact with it before, but never had time to really invest in it. Since then I’ve been into Programming and Data Science, I took some courses on Python, Data Analysis and Machine Learning and I also hold some certificates (check them out). My last achievement is a master degree in Finance and Information Technology where I could combine my previous experiences and learn a lot about technology and data science.

Currently, I’m working as a data scientist in a project called “Mapa dos Grupos Armados do Rio de Janeiro”, where I’m responsible for developing machine learning models in the text classification domain(NLP). My role goes from text processing (text extraction, tokenization, normalisation, etc), feature extraction and feature selection to training and evaluating the models. In addition, I also maintain my study routine and have been studying Deep Learning (mainly Keras and TensorFlow), algorithms, containers and I’m eager to go further in MLOps. Also, I have some fun in my personal projects and writing about some topics I have learnt.

Before my shift, I had experiences ranging from real estate development to leading the Forensic Accounting team at one of LATAM’s largest pension funds, where we were the first department to implement Robotic Process Automation (RPA) to automate part of our business workflow. In addition, I have a BA in Economics, an MBA in Corporate Finance.
