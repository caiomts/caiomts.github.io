Applying Tidy Data from scratch to financial statements

Applying Tidy Data from scratch to financial statements Introduction It might be counter-intuitive, and financial practitioners can be shocked, but the best way to deal with a large amount of financial statements is pivoting them around the date. The aim here is to put you in touch with the Tidy Data framework. Applying it with Python to the Financial Statements of publicly traded Brazilian companies, I’ll show you why this approach saves time and effort and should be used to prepare data for analysis.
