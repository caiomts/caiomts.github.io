About Me

Hi, I’m Caio.

I’m passionate about the open source world, programming, data science and A.I. I do believe that data science can help any businesses, including those owned by normal people to succeed. However I’m also excited on how data science and A.I. can improve our daily life. Of course, those evolutions come together with a bunch of concerns about privacy and bias that I’m also very sensible about.

Professionally-wise, I have more than eight years of experience ranging from Real Estate development to Head of Forensic Accounting in one of the biggest pension funds in LATAM. Throughout all these experiences, I relied on data to make my business decisions. In addition, I hold a BA in Economics, an MBA in Corporate Finance, and I’m a Master student in Finance and Information Technology.

About three years ago I dived into Data Science and then I realized I have always been using some Data Science techniques throughout my career. Currently, I started working as a freelance Data Scientist on a 14-month project. I’m using NLP Models to classify anonymous witness reports in an amazing project of great social interest for an NGO in Brazil. In addition, I’m sharpening my knowledge in ML (Deep Learning with Keras and TensorFlow), diving into Big Data (Spark and No-SQL) and automating boring stuff with Python.

If you want to know more about me and my work, take a look at my portfolio project, follow me on social media or reach out to me by e-mail.
