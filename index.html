About

Hi, I’m Caio and I’m a Data Scientist. Data Science is a powerful tool and most normal businesses, owned by normal people, can benefit from simple data solutions. Forget fancy models that no one can explain. Data Science is not Rocket Science. My goals are to demystify it and to solve real business problems, turning data into strategy, planning and action! (And I don’t need to wear a suit to do it.)

I have more than eight years of professional experience ranging from real estate development to Head of Forensic Accounting in one of the biggest pension funds in LATAM. throughout all these experiences, I relied on data to make my business decisions. In addition, I hold a BA in Economics, an MBA in Corporate Finance, and I’m a Master student in Finance and Information Technology.

About three years ago I dived into Data Science and then I realized two things. It is not an end, but a means and I always have been using some Data Science approaches throughout my career. I can use data to predict sales, pricing or accounts receivable and inventory turnovers, but also to raise insights about clients or to define business strategy and financial planning. Let’s make right decisions, data-driven decisions. As long as the data is available, we can use it, but if it’s not, why not start tracking your own data right now?

If you want to know more about me and my work, take a look at my portfolio project, follow me on social media or reach out to me by e-mail.
